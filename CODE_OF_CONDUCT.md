# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on project goals and technical quality

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement Responsibilities

The maintainer is responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Reporting

Please open a "Legal or compliance notice" issue template for moderation concerns, or contact the maintainer through GitHub profile channels.
